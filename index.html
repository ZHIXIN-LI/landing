Introduction

Honour of Kings is a fantasy role-playing "multiplayer online battle arena" game. The app is free to download, but players pay to upgrade their characters or costumes to help them advance to the next level. It is the highest-grossing mobile game of all time as well as the most downloaded app globally and one of the highest-grossing game. There are four types of damage and five major positions in this game.

1. Physical Damage

It is the most popular damage in the game, it appears with red-colour damage numbers. Physical damage can be decreased by armor.

2. Magical Damage

It appears with blue-colour damage numbers. Magical damage can be decreased by magical resistance.

3. Real Damage

It is the most powerful damage. It cannot be blocked by shields, it can be only decreased by damage-decrease buffs.

4. Critical Damage

It deals massive damage in crit chance(percentage %), it can be physical, magical or real damage.
